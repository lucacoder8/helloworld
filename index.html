Normal Headroom Hoist

Mounted to single girder cranes when there are no specific requirements about lifting heights.

STANDARD FEATURES 

  • Capacity: from 0.8 to 50 tonnes
  • Duty mode (FEM): from 1 Am to 3 m
  • Reeving: 2/1, 4/1
  • Lifting speed: V1 and V2
  • Lifting height: from 7 to 32 m

Electric Chain Hoist

Electric chain hoists incorporate precision-engineered components including motor assemblies, coupling mechanisms, electromagnetic brake systems, multi-stage gearing, and reinforced hook blocks that deliver reliable lifting performance. These units handle light to medium loads ranging from 0.5 to 2 tonnes capacity, making them ideal for frequent lifting operations in manufacturing environments.

Electric options provide ease of operation through push-button controls and remote operation capabilities, while low maintenance requirements and compact design ensure minimal operational disruption. Available trolley configurations include motorised, plain, or gear trolley options, allowing customization based on specific application requirements and workspace constraints.
