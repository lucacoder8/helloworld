High-Precision CNC Craft

From micro features to monolithic aerospace structures, Rosye fuses leading-edge machine tools, disciplined process engineering, and realtime data to create parts that pass the harshest inspections. Every detail of our workflow centers on stability, repeatability, and scientific control.

Five Pillars Of Technical Excellence

Thermal Intelligence

Closed-loop HVAC, coolant chilling, and machine warm-up protocols keep our work envelope within ±0.2 °C, eliminating thermal drift on 30-hour cycles.

Toolpath Science

Hybrid CAM workflows combine trochoidal milling, adaptive roughing, and finish blending for mirror surfaces and extended tool life in exotic alloys.

5-Axis Mastery

Palletized 5-axis cells with dynamic work offsets allow us to hit multiple datums in one clamping, minimizing stack-up and maximizing geometric fidelity.

Metrology First

Renishaw probing, laser tool setters, and Zeiss CMM validation feed measurement data back to the CAM team for live adjustments.

Surface Integrity

We manage chip load, harmonic vibration, and coolant chemistry to produce burr-free edges, optical finishes, and consistent Ra values.

Process Documentation

Each routing includes torque specs, inspection checkpoints, fixturing notes, and revision controls to ensure copies remain faithful to the original run.

Machine Suite

5-Axis Machining Centers

Hermle C 42 | Makino D500 | Grob G350—equipped with 120-tool magazines, 18k spindles, and inline probing for seamless unattended production.

Swiss Turning & Micro Milling

Citizen L series plus Kern micro mills produce miniature components with micro bores down to 0.3 mm and wire-EDM integration for ultra-fine features.

EDM & Grinding

Sinker, wire, and laser EDM backed by creep-feed grinding handle conductive superalloys, mold inserts, and sharp inside corners better than traditional milling.

Quality Workflows

Incoming Materials

Lot verification, PMI testing, and digital material cert storage ensure supply-chain integrity and traceability.

In-Process Control

SPC monitoring, inline probing, and machine alarms respond instantly if any dimension trends toward limits.

Final Verification

Zeiss CMM, optical scanning, surface profilometry, and laser trackers compile a comprehensive FAIR that travels with each shipment.

Continuous Improvement

Cross-functional reviews after every launch capture lessons, update routings, and feed our knowledge base for future programs.
