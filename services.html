Our capabilities

Services engineered for delicate objects, demanding schedules, and VIP treatment.

MAHH combines senior project managers, specialized crews, and real-time oversight tools so your relocation feels precise rather than chaotic.

Residence Transitions

Concierge-level packing, art and piano handling, closet cataloging, and white-glove placement in your new space.

  • Custom crating for heirlooms, sculptures, and designer lighting.
  • Protective materials for flooring, walls, elevator interiors.
  • Unpacking with wardrobe steaming, kitchen staging, and eco-friendly recycling.

Corporate Mobility

Enterprise-level relocations synchronized with IT, facilities, and security teams to avoid downtime.

  • Server-room shutdown, backup, and re-rack services with audit trails.
  • Asset tagging for every workstation, chair, and collaboration device.
  • Night and weekend changeovers, including deep clean and reset.

Hospitality & Retail

Soft-opening installs, flagship refreshes, and pop-up activations with millimetric precision.

  • Fixture receiving, inspection, and staged deployment.
  • Coordinated labor with visual merchandisers and construction partners.
  • 24-hour security cleared crews for premium shopping districts.

Event Logistics

Turnkey support for exhibitions, summits, and brand previews, including transport, setup, and load-out.

  • Integrated scheduling with venue operations and audiovisual vendors.
  • On-site captains to manage credentials, staging flows, and strike lists.
  • Secure storage for props, crates, and signage between tour stops.

Special Handling

Museum-grade transport for art, lab gear, and collectibles, complete with climate data logging.

  • Shock sensors, tilt indicators, and independent GPS tracking.
  • Dedicated technicians for calibration-sensitive assets.
  • Insurance documentation produced before dispatch.

Storage Solutions

Flexible holding periods in our monitored warehouses with concierge delivery back to your location.

  • RFID-labeled containers for instant retrieval.
  • Temperature and humidity controlled vault rooms.
  • Inventory reports available via secure client portal.

Methodology

Briefing, execution, and confirmation live inside a disciplined framework.

  1. Discovery: virtual or in-person walk-through with itemized scope, building rules, and risk mapping.
  2. Design: route planning, crew composition, vehicle selection, and material staging.
  3. Deployment: supervisors directing barcode checklists while coordinators update stakeholders.
  4. Finale: placement, QC, debris removal, and after-action report within 12 hours.
