Our story

MAHH is a collective of logisticians, artisans, and hospitality leaders reinventing what moving feels like.

We built MAHH to be the partner that luxury homeowners, creative studios, and multinational brands call when precision matters more than speed.

Legacy of mastery

MAHH began as a boutique white-glove service in 2006. Two decades later, we operate across continents with a core team that still insists on handwritten checklists, immaculate uniforms, and direct accountability.

We are proud of our industry-first mentorship program that pairs new crew members with senior art handlers, ensuring that our culture of respect and calm expertise is preserved.

Numbers that matter

  • 92% of clients return for future projects or recommend us internally.
  • 120 training hours per crew member annually.
  • Zero significant claims in the past 36 months.

People-first culture

We invest in wellness, continuing education, and leadership pathways so that the person walking into your residence or boardroom is confident, courteous, and empowered to solve problems.

Technology with tact

Our MoveSync platform tracks inventory, building timings, permits, and updates without overwhelming you, delivering meaningful information at just the right moment.

Sustainability in action

Reusable crates, hybrid vehicles, and donation partnerships ensure that moving day reduces waste instead of generating it.
