Heritage

Crafting serene moves through hospitality roots, logistics expertise, and people-first culture.

ZLSD launched in 2006 when boutique hoteliers partnered with logistics veterans to reimagine how relocation should feel. Today, our employee-owned company spans coast-to-coast crews with a singular promise: protect every milestone as if it were our own.

Operating hubs

  • New York City: Luxury residential and art services
  • Austin: Tech campus and headquarters transitions
  • Seattle: Innovation labs and global freight gateway
  • Partner agents in 26 international markets

Milestones

2006

Founded with two trucks and a promise to deliver hotel-grade courtesy to every move. Early clientele included boutique residences across Manhattan.

2012

Expanded to Austin and Seattle, launching the Care In Motion training academy and investing in GPS-enabled fleet technology.

2018

Introduced international concierge desk, bonded warehouses, and a digital client portal to oversee complex assignments worldwide.

Today

Employee-owned teams execute more than 600 relocations a year while maintaining a 98 percent on-time record and 4.9-star satisfaction rating.

Values in motion

Innovation

We continually test lift technology, data-driven scheduling, and collaboration tools that shorten timelines and enhance visibility.
