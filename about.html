Who we are

Watson builds modern CNC operations where craftspeople and automation thrive together.

Founded in 1987, Watson evolved from a family tool-and-die shop into a digital-first manufacturing partner trusted by global OEMs.

Mission & values

Our mission is to remove friction between design intent and manufactured reality. We invest heavily in people, process automation, and quality culture so customers can focus on innovation.

Relentless precision

Every machinist is cross-trained on programming, inspection, and maintenance to close the loop between problem and solution.

Radical transparency

We share throughput data, downtime logs, and quality metrics directly with customers through secure dashboards.

Sustainable impact

Coolant recycling, energy monitoring, and additive tooling cut waste and support ISO 14001 initiatives.

Fast facts

  • 85,000 sq ft smart factory in Austin, Texas
  • AS9100D, ISO 13485, ITAR registered
  • 26 multi-axis machining centers
  • Dedicated Class 7 cleanroom for medical finishing
  • Average customer partnership: 9.4 years
