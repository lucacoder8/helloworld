About Rosye

For more than twenty years, Rosye has been the quiet force behind breakthrough aircraft, life changing surgical platforms, and frontier energy systems. We turn ambitious ideas into tangible components by blending advanced technology with an obsessive, craft driven culture.

Our Story

We started as a boutique shop supporting Silicon Valley labs. Today Rosye operates a 38,000 sq.ft. smart factory powered by data, automation, and an expert CNC team.

People

Over 120 specialists spanning machinists, process engineers, project managers, and quality analysts. Continuous learning and cross training keep us adaptable.

Culture

We balance artisan pride with scientific rigor. Every shift begins with alignment meetings, and every job closes with a retrospective so we improve relentlessly.

Technology

Digital twins of our machining cells, automated pallet pools, and MES dashboards give clients live visibility into progress and quality checkpoints.

Community & Sustainability

Apprenticeships

Rosye sponsors paid training that pairs veteran machinists with aspiring talent, ensuring the trade thrives for the next generation.

Responsible Manufacturing

Coolant recycling, solar assisted power, and closed loop material handling reduce our environmental footprint.

STEM Outreach

We mentor robotics teams and donate CNC time to nonprofits that inspire students to pursue engineering careers.
