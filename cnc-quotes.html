Transparent CNC Quoting

Rosye combines seasoned estimators with algorithmic costing to deliver accurate pricing, manufacturability feedback, and production schedules within 24 hours. No black boxes, no surprises.

What To Send

Equip us with the right data and we return a technically grounded offer.

Design Package

3D model (STEP, Parasolid) + fully dimensioned PDF prints. Include tolerances, finish callouts, and special notes.

Build Intent

Specify annual volumes, lot sizes, target delivery dates, and quality requirements such as FAI, PPAP, or cleanroom packaging.

Context

Share functional goals, mating components, or risk areas so our engineers can recommend the ideal process plan.

Quote Flow

01 Intake

Secure upload portal, NDA-ready. Automated checks validate files and assign the right manufacturing cell lead.

02 Engineering Review

Process engineers analyze tolerances, simulate toolpaths, and flag DFM opportunities for cost, lead time, or reliability gains.

03 Pricing & Schedule

We blend machine-hour models with historical benchmarks to produce precise, component-level pricing plus delivery commitments.

04 Collaborative Kickoff

We host a call or send annotated notes summarizing risks, critical-to-quality features, and action items prior to PO release.
